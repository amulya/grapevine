About

Today, our world is more interconnected than ever. But the more global connections form, the less personal connections develop. One way personal connections were maintained even across the world was through a pen pal system. The concept of pen pals isn't new; some may even call it outdated. But now we've revolutionized it using the power of the Internet. Pen pals are no longer limited to students lucky enough to go to a school that supports a pen pal program. Now, anyone can get a pen pal at a moment's notice. Just click sign up to reach out to someone new today.
